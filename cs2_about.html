ABOUT ME

-- How I cued up my love for billiards --

My Life

Personally, as a student, having billiards as my hobby has its perks, allowing me to connect with friends , relieve stress, and sharpen my concentration, all while enjoying a fun and challenging game. However, this sport also has its challenges because I have to learn how to balance my time between practice and schoolwork. You see, I am a student who studies at a prestigious highschool which has high standards. Which also means that I have schoolworks that are loaded, waiting to be done. But despite the "challenge" or "availability" that I have, the fact that I always meet new people, learn new things, and learn strategizing for the love of the game, allows me to relieve all the loads that I have on my shoulders; we can say that at this point, this game is already my stress reliever during my breaks.

How I Started

I started playing billiards during the quarantine during the pandemic of Covid-19. You can say that I have been playing this game for quite some time now. I was introduced in this game by my cousins who influenced me into playing the game which later on, contributed to my love for the sport.

If you're wondering whats pushing me into loving this sport, it is simply a masterpiece. For me, billiards is more than a game. It's fun, fast, and full of little strategies that keep me thinking. I love the mix of focus and relaxation it gives me as I play the game with many different people. Beyond the game itself, billiards is about connection. Every match I've played has been a way to bond, laugh, and sometimes compete, but still always enjoyable.

My Journey

These days, I play billiards at least once or twice a week, at a local pool table, as I am only available during the two-day weekends. I usually play with my friends, but sometimes I just practice on my own. This practice had become a regular part of my routine. So far, I have been working on improving my cue control, especially with draw shots as personally, they are the toughest shots. Until now, every game still teaches me something new about the angles, timing, and positioning. In addition, out of all the versions of billiards, my favorite is the 10-ball.



Billiards isn't just a game to me, it is a passion that I will forever carry.

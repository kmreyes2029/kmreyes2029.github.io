GAMES

-- Different Billiard Games --

Pool Games

  • 8-Ball

Generally the most popular. 15 object balls + cue ball. The goal is to sink your group (solids or stripes) then lastly the 8-ball.

  • 9-Ball

Unlike 8-ball, this is only played with balls 1 to 9. You must always hit the lowest-numbered ball first. You can win by pocketing the 9.

  • 10-Ball

10-ball is 5 balls shorter than 8-ball. It is similar to 9-ball, but uses balls 1 to 10. It is considered more challenging.

Snooker

Snooker is played on a larger 12' by 6' table with 21 balls. Players score by potting the red balls worth 1 point while the colored balls are worth 2-7 points. It is said to be the most strategic game of pool.

Carom Billiards

Carom Billiards is a unique game of billiard. It is played on a pocketless 10' by 5' table with 3 balls. The goal is to hit both object balls with the cue ball in one shot.
